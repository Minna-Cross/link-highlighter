# Privacy Information

Link Highlighter requires access to your browser history to function properly.

## What data we access:
- **URL visit history**: To determine when you last visited each link
- **Visit timestamps**: To calculate how recently you visited

## What we do NOT access:
- Page content
- Personal information
- Passwords or form data

## Data handling:
- All processing happens locally in your browser
- No data is sent to external servers
- Visit data is only used for highlighting links
- No permanent storage of your history data
